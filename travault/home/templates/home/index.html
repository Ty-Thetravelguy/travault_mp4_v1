Features

Client Management

Store and manage detailed client data, including travel preferences, contact information, and spend history. Easily access and update client data to provide personalized service.

Supplier Database

Maintain a robust database of suppliers, including airlines, hotels, car rental companies, ground transport providers & rail. Store contract details, negotiated rates, payment information and performance metrics for quick reference.

Ticket Logging and Tracking

Efficiently log and track client requests, inquiries, and issues. Our ticket system ensures that no client need goes unaddressed, improving response times and client satisfaction.

Agent Support Management

Store detailed information fto support your travel consultants. Load and update agent support contact information, and supplier processess

Document Storage and Retrieval

Securely store and easily retrieve important documents such as client contracts, supplier agreements, and travel itineraries. Our intuitive filing system ensures that you have the right information at your fingertips.

Customizable Reporting

Generate insightful reports on client trends, supplier performance, and consultant productivity. Use data-driven insights to make informed business decisions and identify growth opportunities.

Task Management and Reminders

Set and track tasks related to client requests, follow-ups, and administrative duties. Automatic reminders help ensure that no important deadlines or client needs are overlooked.

Communication Logs

Record all client interactions, including calls, emails, and meetings. This comprehensive communication history enables seamless collaboration among team members and ensures consistent client service.

Customizable Fields and Workflows

Tailor the CRM to your agency's specific needs with customizable fields and workflows. Adapt the system as your business grows and evolves.

Data Security and Compliance

Protect sensitive client and business data with robust security measures. Our system helps you maintain compliance with data protection regulations in the travel industry.

Integration Capabilities

Seamlessly integrate with other tools such as email platforms, accounting software, and GDS (Global Distribution Systems) to streamline your operations and reduce data duplication.

Mobile Access

Access critical client and supplier information on-the-go with our mobile-friendly interface, allowing consultants to provide top-notch service from anywhere.

About Travault

From Travel Agent to Tech Innovator

Meet Our Founder

With 20 years in the travel sector, Tyron has navigated every aspect of the industry. From retail beginnings to corporate travel management, and now serving in operations for a travel management company, Tyron's journey has provided a comprehensive understanding of the challenges faced by travel businesses of all sizes.

The Travault Vision

During his years in the industry, Tyron identified a critical gap in the market: smaller travel management companies were struggling with expensive CRM systems that often exceeded their needs and budgets. This realization sparked an idea - to create a CRM solution tailored specifically for boutique travel management companies in the corporate travel sector.

Bridging Technology and Travel Expertise

Combining his deep industry knowledge with newfound coding skills, Tyron embarked on creating Travault as his final project. This CRM system is designed to meet the unique needs of smaller travel management companies, offering robust functionality without the hefty price tag of larger systems.

Our Mission

At Travault, our mission is clear: to provide boutique travel management companies with a powerful, user-friendly CRM system at a fraction of the cost of traditional solutions. We believe that with the right tools, smaller companies can compete effectively in the corporate travel market, delivering exceptional service to their clients without breaking the bank.

Looking to the Future

As we continue to develop and refine Travault, we remain committed to our core values of affordability, efficiency, and industry-specific functionality. We're excited to be part of the journey in modernizing travel management technology and supporting the success of boutique travel management companies around the world.

Join us in revolutionizing the way boutique travel management companies operate in the corporate travel sector!
