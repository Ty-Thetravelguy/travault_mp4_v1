Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 August 2024 Last Updated: 26 August 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how TraVault collects, uses, and protects your personal data. By using our service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Data Collection

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, email address, phone number, and payment details.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use the service, including login times, pages viewed, and actions taken.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, and device information.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our CRM services.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Communicate with you about your account and provide customer support.
  • Analyse usage trends to enhance the user experience.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted partners who assist us in operating our service, processing payments, or conducting business activities. All such partners are required to maintain the confidentiality of your data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers, which are protected by industry-standard security measures. We implement a variety of security technologies and procedures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to certain legal obligations.
  • Withdraw consent to data processing where consent is the basis of processing.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our platform. Cookies help us understand your preferences and improve service functionality. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. After this period, your data will be securely deleted.

9. Third-Party Services

Our service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. International Transfers

If we transfer your data outside the UK, we will ensure that adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with UK data protection laws.

11. Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a data breach, we will promptly notify affected users and take necessary steps to mitigate any potential harm. We will also comply with legal requirements for reporting data breaches to the relevant authorities.
